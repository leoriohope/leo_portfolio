About Me I'm currently a Computer Science PhD student and a Graduate Research Assitant at the University of Central Florida in Orlando, FL. My advisor is Dr. Carolina Cruz-Neira.

Get to know me!

Hi! It's Liangding Li and I am a current PhD student in Computer Science. My background focuses on applying Machine Learning and Generative AI in Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) to enhance immersive experiences and interaction capabilities.

My expertise includes data structures, algorithms, and practical development in Unity 3D, with a strong emphasis on leveraging computer vision, NLP deep learning, and AI-driven object tracking to create intelligent, adaptive VR/AR environments. I am particularly interested in how GenAI can be integrated into real-time simulations, digital twins, and spatial computing, unlocking new possibilities for education, training, and human-computer interaction.
